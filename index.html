How the Scoring Works

Crispiness

The ideal fry should have a satisfying crunch that gives way to a fluffy interior. Perfectly crispy fries enhance the overall eating experience, making each bite enjoyable without being overly greasy or soggy.

Flavor

Fast food fries should have a rich, satisfying taste with just the right amount of seasoning. Whether simply salted or featuring unique spices, they should enhance the natural potato flavor and keep you coming back for more.

Consistency

Consistent fries in size and shape ensure even cooking, which contributes to a uniform taste and texture. This quality helps maintain a satisfying crunch throughout, allowing for a reliable experience every time.

Texture

The best fries strike a perfect balance between a crispy exterior and a soft, tender interior. This contrast creates a delightful mouthfeel, making each bite a pleasure as the fries provide both crunch and comfort.
